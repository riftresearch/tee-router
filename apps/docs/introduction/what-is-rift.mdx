title: "What is Rift?"
---

**Rift** is a non custodial Bitcoin trading protocol with best-in-class rates and security for market and limit orders.

Unlike other cross-chain protocols that rely on multi-sigs or staking for security, Rift uses cloud TEEs to enforce protocol rules for escrow and release of funds. This means Rift has:

- **The best rates for users** — Deep liquidity and the best rates on popular routes like cbBTC↔BTC, ETH↔BTC, and USDC↔BTC. Fees start at 8 bips.
- **Self-custody cross-chain swaps** — Users stay in full control of their funds. It's impossible for Rift to freeze or steal user funds.
- **Minimal attack surface** — There is no validator set to corrupt or smart contracts to exploit.
- **100% open source protocol code** — Anyone can audit our [protocol code](https://github.com/riftresearch/tee-otc) to verify how it works.
